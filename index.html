📜 How to Play

🎯 The Goal

Build a thriving menagerie of monsters. Capture them in the wild, keep them alive and happy, breed rare combinations, and chase legendary variants like Gold, Void, Crystal, and Rainbow.

🕸 Capturing Monsters

Press ✦ Capture Wild Monster to find a wild monster and play a mini-game to earn its trust:

  • Uncommon / Rare — Feed Game: Food items fall from the top. Click the one matching the monster's thought bubble. Complete 2–3 rounds to capture it.
  • Epic / Legendary — Rhythm Game: Watch a button sequence light up, then repeat it exactly. One wrong press and the monster flees.
  • Rarer monsters have faster timing and more rounds.
  • Legendary monsters can never be caught in the wild — they only come from breeding.

💚 Keeping Monsters Alive

All four stats drain every 4 seconds. If Health hits zero, the monster vanishes forever.

  • Feed — Restores Hunger. Triggers a munching animation and sound.
  • Play — Boosts Happiness. Costs a little Energy. Requires 15+ Energy to use.
  • Rest — Puts the monster to sleep. Energy recovers slowly (about 2 minutes to fully recharge from zero). Hunger and Happiness still drain slowly while asleep. Press again to wake them.
  • Heal — Restores Health directly.

A monster with low Hunger or Energy loses Health much faster. Don't let them stay starved or exhausted for long.

⚗ Breeding

  • Only Adult monsters can breed (age 501+). Check the stage badge on each monster.
  • Select two Adults in the Breeding Pit, then press ⚗ Breed Monsters.
  • Rarer parents increase the odds of rarer offspring. Epic × Epic will often produce Epic or better results.
  • Legendary variants (Gold, Void, Crystal) require at least one Legendary parent.
  • Rainbow requires both parents to be Legendary — the rarest outcome in the game.

⬆ XP & Levels

Monsters earn XP from feeding (+8), playing (+12), healing (+6), sleeping (+3/tick), and aging (+1/tick). Levelling up plays a chime and is tracked toward achievements. The level badge appears next to the monster's name.

✨ Achievements & Accessories

Achievements unlock automatically as you play — capturing milestones, breeding goals, level targets, and more. Each achievement unlocks a cosmetic Accessory (Crown, Scarf, Halo, Shades, Bow Tie, Flower Crown). Open ✨ Accessories to equip one on your active monster.

🌿 Habitats & Challenges

  • Habitats: Drag monsters from the Wild Pen into a themed zone. Matching habitat = small Happiness bonus every tick.
  • Daily Challenges: Three new goals refresh every midnight. Complete them to earn 🪙 tokens and build a daily streak.

💾 Quick Tips

  • The game auto-saves every 4 seconds — closing the tab won't lose your monsters.
  • Click a monster's name to rename it. Click the app title to rename your whole menagerie.
  • Seasonal monsters only appear at real-world times: ❄ Frost in winter, ☀ Solar at noon–3pm, 🌙 Midnight at 10pm–5am.
  • Use Release to free a monster and make room. Max 12 monsters at a time.
  • Enable 🔔 Alerts to get browser notifications when monsters need attention while the tab is hidden.
